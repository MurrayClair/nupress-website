What We Deliver

Built to withstand
Australia's harshest conditions

When a longwall shearer breaks down, every hour of downtime costs tens of thousands of dollars. Nupress maintains rapid-response capability for mining breakdown situations, with experienced machinists available when you need them most.

  • Drill Bits & Drilling Components Precision-ground cutting tools for surface and underground drilling
  • Wear Parts & Liners Custom wear plates and liners in Hardox and AR-grade steels
  • Crusher Components Manganese steel crusher liners, bowl liners, and mantle sections
  • Conveyor & Material Handling Idler components, head pulleys, sprockets, and chain drives
  • Longwall & Continuous Miner Parts Shearer drum components, ranging arms, and cutting heads
  • Reverse Engineering Obsolete or legacy parts replicated from physical samples
