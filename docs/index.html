About Us

Engineering Excellence Since 1972

For over five decades, Nupress Group has been at the forefront of Australian precision engineering. Founded in 1972 in Newcastle, New South Wales, we have grown from a specialist machining shop into a globally recognised engineering group serving six distinct industry sectors.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility houses an exclusively Mazak CNC machine fleet, enabling us to deliver precision components with unmatched consistency and quality. With our head office and manufacturing base in Newcastle, Australia, and an office in Toronto, Canada, we serve customers worldwide.

We embrace 5S methodology and LEAN manufacturing principles to continuously improve our processes, eliminate waste, and deliver maximum value to our customers.

"To provide engineered components and products to a global customer base, enabling their visions to become reality."

Capabilities

End-to-End Engineering Solutions

From initial design through to finished product, Nupress delivers comprehensive manufacturing capabilities under one roof.

Precision CNC Machining

Our exclusively Mazak CNC machine fleet delivers multi-axis turning, milling, and machining centre operations with exceptional accuracy and repeatability across all material types.

Engineering & Design

Full CAD/CAM/CAE capability for product development, design optimisation, and reverse engineering. We transform concepts into production-ready designs efficiently.

Castings & Forgings

Supply and machining of castings and forgings in a wide range of ferrous and non-ferrous materials, sourced from our trusted supply chain partners.

Welding & Fabrication

Qualified welding and fabrication services including TIG, MIG, and specialist welding processes to demanding codes and standards.

Assemblies & Finishing

Complete sub-assembly and finishing services including surface treatments, painting, anodising, and final product assembly ready for delivery.

Quality Assurance

AS9100 and ISO 9001 certified quality management. CMM inspection, full material traceability, and comprehensive test documentation for every project.

Our Facility

Advanced Cyber Manufacturing

Our Newcastle facility integrates cutting-edge digital technologies with precision manufacturing for optimum efficiency and quality.

Offline Programming

Advanced CAM software for offline CNC program development and verification, maximising machine uptime and reducing setup times.

Machine Simulation

Full 3D simulation of machining processes to verify toolpaths, detect collisions, and optimise cycle times before cutting metal.

Production Scheduling

Integrated digital scheduling systems ensure efficient resource allocation, on-time delivery, and transparent production tracking.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live machine monitoring across the shop floor provides real-time visibility of production status, machine utilisation, and performance.

Sandvik Tooling Crib Partnership

Our partnership with Sandvik provides a managed tooling crib solution, ensuring optimal cutting tool availability, cost management, and access to the latest tooling technology for our Mazak fleet.

Reverse Engineering

Full reverse engineering capability across four disciplines: metrology (precision measurement), metallurgy (material analysis), manufacturing (CAD/CAM/CAE reproduction), and software integration for seamless digital workflows.
