Specialist facade engineering
for landmark projects

Nupress Facades designs and installs architectural glass and facade systems that define the visual identity of landmark buildings worldwide. From glass fin walls and tension structures to overhead glazing, glass stairs, and bespoke entrance hardware — we engineer solutions that push the boundaries of what is architecturally and structurally possible. With operations in Australia and North America, we deliver global capability with local expertise.

What We Deliver

Precision engineering
meets architectural vision

Nupress Facades brings manufacturing precision to the architectural world. Every system is engineered with the same rigour we apply to aerospace components — because when glass is structural, precision is everything. We operate across Australia and North America, delivering landmark facade projects of any scale.

  • Glass Fin Walls Structural glass fins providing lateral support for fully glazed facades
  • Tension Structures High-tensile cable and rod assemblies for dramatic architectural expressions
  • Metal Fin & Steel Structures Architectural metal fins, steel frames, and bespoke structural assemblies
  • Overhead Glazed Solutions Structural glazed roofs, skylights, and canopy systems
  • Glass Stairs & Walkways Structural glass treads, balustrades, and walkway systems
  • Clear Span Glass & Entrances Frameless entrances, all-glass automatic doors, and clear-span glazed walls
  • Hardware & Custom Solutions Precision CNC-machined fixings, patch fittings, spider assemblies, and bespoke facade hardware
