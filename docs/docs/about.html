Our Story

Built on decades of
manufacturing expertise

Nupress was founded in 1972 in Newcastle, NSW — a city with deep roots in industrial manufacturing and engineering excellence. What began as a precision engineering workshop has evolved over five decades into a multi-division advanced manufacturing company serving the world's most demanding industries.

Today, our Cardiff facility houses an extensive fleet of CNC lathes, mill-turn centres, VMCs, HMCs, 5-axis simultaneous machining centres, and wire EDM — all operating under AS9100D and ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management systems. We deliver complete parts and assemblies, not just individual components.

Across five specialist divisions — Aerospace & Defence, Facades, Mining & Minerals, Energy, and General Engineering — Nupress delivers components and assemblies that must perform without compromise in applications where failure is not an option. With operations extending to North America, Nupress is positioned as a trusted global manufacturing partner.
